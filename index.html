A DOWNTOWN

PORTLAND RESTAURANT

This is a tavern for the modern ages — spacious and warm, serving Northwest-inspired American dishes with ample use of local, organic ingredients. The inviting atmosphere of our downtown Portland restaurant includes touches small and big, like a 14-seat communal table made from the vertical cross-section of a walnut tree. Open for every eating occasion of the day, from breakfast to late-night snacking and imbibing, Red Star Tavern is a hot spot worth dropping by any time thirst or hunger strikes. Our downtown Portland restaurant is particularly buzzing during the epic happy hour, which runs over 5 hours every day, from 2:00 p.m. - 6:00 p.m.
